2019–2021

Renewable Electricity Expansion

Scaling clean electricity and efficiency across global sites.

Energy

What changed

  • Set a goal of 100% renewable electricity globally by 2030 and began accelerating purchases to get there.
  • Expanded facility energy conservation projects toward a 4 billion kWh cumulative savings by 2030.
  • Reported 37.9 billion kWh of renewable electricity supply and purchases across five years.
  • Advanced green-building practices by targeting LEED® certification for new factories and facilities.
  • Built playbooks with sites and utilities to increase access to renewable power contracts.

2022

Water Stewardship

Restoration projects and water reuse to support watershed health.

Water

What changed

  • Committed to net positive water by 2030 by conserving 60 billion gallons and funding restoration.
  • Funded 40+ watershed restoration projects across multiple regions.
  • By end of 2023, reported ~13 billion gallons conserved and restored toward the goal.
  • Scaled on-site reuse and conservation alongside external projects in water-stressed basins.
  • Published annual water reports with project-level details and progress.

2023

Zero Waste Progress

Circularity, supplier engagement, and responsible handling.

Waste

What changed

  • Targeted zero waste to landfill by 2030 and circular economy strategies for 60% of manufacturing waste.
  • Reported sending just ~4% of waste to landfill by end of 2023.
  • In 2024, applied circular practices (reuse, recovery, recycling) to ~66% of manufacturing waste streams.
  • Expanded supplier partnerships to design for reuse and reduce packaging impacts.
  • Maintained high recycling rates for non-hazardous waste across sites.

2024

Climate Action Roadmap

Operational emissions, supplier ties, and product efficiency.

Climate

What changed

  • Net-zero Scope 1 & 2 by 2040, with a 2030 interim goal to cut absolute Scope 1 & 2 by 10% vs. 2019.
  • 99% renewable electricity achieved in 2023; continued progress in 2024.
  • Roadmap includes novel abatement tech for F-GHGs/N2O, chemistry substitution, and tool optimization.
  • New facilities targeted to USGBC LEED® standards to reduce operational impacts.
  • Value-chain programs like Catalyze and the Semiconductor Climate Consortium to accelerate supplier renewables.

2030

2030 Ambitions

Energy efficiency, renewable coverage, responsible manufacturing.

Energy

What changes

  • 100% renewable electricity globally and 4B kWh cumulative energy savings.
  • Net positive water: conserve 60B gallons and fund restoration projects.
  • Zero waste to landfill and circular strategies for 60% of manufacturing waste.
  • Supply chain: reduce Scope 3 supply-chain emissions by 30% from a 2021 baseline.
  • Products: improve microprocessor energy efficiency by 10x and cut platform reference design footprint by 30%+.

2040+

Long-Term Net-Zero Vision

Efficiency, clean energy, and resilient ecosystems.

Vision

What continues

  • Net-zero Scope 1 & 2 by 2040; maintain 100% renewable electricity across operations.
  • Net-zero upstream Scope 3 by 2050 with sustained supplier engagement.
  • Deploy novel abatement with higher destruction removal efficiency as technologies mature.
  • Electrify on-site and leased vehicle fleets where feasible; transition to low-GWP heat transfer fluids.
  • Continue building to LEED® standards and scaling energy conservation programs.
  • Deepen collaboration via Catalyze and SCC to expand renewables across the value chain.
